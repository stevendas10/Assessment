Dear Sir/Madam,Thank you for your inquiry.

If your worker is operating within the debugger but not while submitting a query to the website, kindly verify the following:

  • Make sure the Worker is deployed on your domain to the required route.
  • Make sure the route pattern corresponds to the URL structure of your website.
  • Verify that the Cloudflare dashboard's Worker script is correctly stored and enabled.

For additional help, kindly provide your Worker script and the route settings if the problem continues.
